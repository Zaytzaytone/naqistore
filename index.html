Our oil

We work with growers who harvest early and press within hours—so you taste freshness, not fatigue.

NaqiStore is built around one idea: olive oil should taste like olives—herbaceous, slightly peppery, and clean on the finish. We bottle in small runs and ship quickly so nothing sits on a shelf longer than it should.

Use our extra virgin for finishing vegetables, dipping bread, and dressing salads. For high-heat cooking, use a generous hand knowing you started with real fruit juice, not refined blends.
